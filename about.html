About the Project

A Catalogue For
Teachers & Researchers

The Serious Game Catalogue aims to provide the scientific community, teachers, designers, and Serious Games enthusiasts with a centralized space to search, explore, and share Learning Games according to various pedagogical, technical and gaming criteria.

The catalogue offers an advanced system for classification and automatic indexing and filtering. Previously, the catalog contained over 700 SG entries from three catalogs:

Research Foundation

The Science Behind the Project

PhD Research

Based on Wielfrid MORIE's PhD, supervised by Iza MARFISI, the platform applies cutting-edge research to automate game metadata collection and enrichment.

ADEM Model

The ADEM model automatically extracts information about Learning Games from web pages, identifying and collecting relevant pedagogical and technical data that can then be enriched by users.

LGMD Schema

The LGMD (Learning Games Metadata) schema facilitates the pedagogical and ludic description of Learning Games — the foundation for all filters and metadata fields in the catalogue.

Collaborative Design

The overall design of the platform and its informational organization were the subject of research conducted in collaboration with teachers from France and Côte d'Ivoire, ensuring the platform truly meets the needs of end-users in the fields of education — covering domains such as STEM, languages, history, and social sciences.
